MATC Mail Center

Overview

The Madison Area Technical College Mail Center is commited to the prompt and precise monitoring and distribution of all incoming and outgoing mail and packages at the college. We are also proud to assist the campus community with a variety of other mailing needs. We encourage you to contact us with any questions at all. You will also find information within this website on mailing topics such as addressing, enclosures, envelopes, Interoffice mail, distribution, US mail classifications, specialized delivery services, and more.

Mail Center Faculty Services

Folding/Inserting

  • The mail center can fold and stuff outgoing letters.
  • Our folding machince can handle jobs up to 3 pages and 2 quarterpage inserts.

Metering

  • All outgoing Madison College mail can have postage applied at the mail center.
  • If you wish to have your outgoing mail metered, please provide your MC with the mail at dropoff.
  • All meted mail with have postage totals debited from department account.
  • All metered mail will go out at 3:30pm M-F.
  • All mail dropped off after 3:00pm will be metered the following business day.

Outbound Packages

  • All Madison College related outbound packages can be labeled and shipped at the Mail Center.
  • Please Provide the following information when you drop off your packages.
    • Destination Address
    • Department MC code
    • Preferred carrier, i.e. Fedex, UPS, SPEEDEE
    • Speed of delivery, i.e. next day, two-day, lowest price.
    • Insurance amount/declared value, if applicable.

Inkjetting/Mailpiece Design

  • Injetting is available if you would like a custom design on your envelope.
  • Before creating a design check with the Mail Center manager to ensure your design complies with postal regulations.
  • injetting is available for bulk mailings of non-windowed envelopes.

Address Correction/Hygiene

  • All mailpieces sent must comply with postal redulations. If you have any questions whether your mailpiece is compliant, please contact the Mail Center manager.

Assign/Manage Mailboxes

  • Mailboxes are assigned and maintained by the Mail Center manager and employees.
  • If you need a new mailbox, no longer require a mailbox, or would like a mailbox set up at a different Madison College Campus. Contact the Mail Center Manager

Insured/Certified Mail

  • The Mail Center is able to send Insured and Certified Mail Pieces

For any questions regarding the mail center sevices please contact Kathy Waters at KWATERS@madisoncollege.edu

Campus Deliveries and USPS Pick Up Times
Monday - Friday
  • 9:00am - USPS Mail is recieved and sorted at the mail center
  • 9:30am - Mail is delivered to Foundation Building, South Madison Campus, and West Madison Campus
  • 10:00am - Mail is delivered to departments within Truax Main Campus Building
  • 11:30am - Mail is delivered to Health Building, Human Protective Services Building, Commercial Ave Campus, Downtown Campus, and Foundation Building
  • 1:00pm - Mail is delivered to departments within Truax Main Campus Building
  • 3:30pm - Outgoing USPS mail is picked up from the mail center.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Can I mail personal mail/packages?

  • Anyone at the college is able to mail personal mail/packages.
  • Letters must have postage attached.
  • Flats must weigh under 13 ounces and must have postage attached.
  • Packages must weigh under 1lb. Must have shipping labels attached.
  • The mail center employees can assist you if you are unsure how much postage to attach.
  • Stamps and shipping labels can be purchased at the Campus Bookstore.

Can I have personal mail delivered to the college?

  • Students and staff members without a permanent MATC mailbox are not allowed to have personal mail delivered to the college.
  • Faculty and Staff are asked to not have personal/non-college-related mail delivered to the college to keep mail volume as manageable as possible.

I forgot my mailbox number/combination. What do I do?

  • You can stop by the Mail Center during our hours of operation and one of our friendly employees will be glad to assist you.

How do I get a mailbox as a new faculty/staff member?

  • PSRP and Full-Time Faculty: Your department head should put in for a new mailbox when you are hired. If they have not, please ask them to contact Kathy Waters at KWATERS@madisoncollege.edu
  • Part-time faculty: You may fill out a mailbox request form here.
